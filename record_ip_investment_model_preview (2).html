Model creative project incubation scenarios, estimate streaming economics, test partnership structures, and evaluate whether a release creates aligned upside for both Lockstep and the artist.
Spotify Streams
Known or estimated Spotify stream count for the release.
Spotify Share of Total Streams
If Spotify is 50% of consumption, the model estimates total streams by dividing Spotify streams by 50%.
Total Cash Invested
Recording, marketing, visuals, advances, tour support, or any other cash deployed by both sides combined.
Company Funding %
What percentage of the total cash investment came from Lockstep. The artist funds the remaining percentage.
Distribution Fee
Percentage paid to distribution before the artist/company revenue split. Starts at 15%.
Streaming Rate
Approximate dollars paid per stream. This is a blunt blended estimate, not a precise royalty statement.

Vinyl Cost Per Unit

Fulfillment / Fees Per Sold Unit
Payment processing, platform fees, packaging, shipping subsidy, or other per-sale cost.
Digital Downloads Module
Digital Download Units
Projected number of digital albums or downloads sold. Defaults to 0.
Digital Download Price
Price per digital download. Defaults to $9.99. No costs or expenses are applied.

Simple Deal Read

If company profit is negative, the deal needs one of these: less company cash invested, more company revenue share, better streaming performance, additional revenue streams, or a strategic reason to accept a streaming loss.

If both company and artist profit are positive, the deal structure is healthier because both sides are winning on the same release.
